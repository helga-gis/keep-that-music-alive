Keep That Music Alive

Move your hands across the zones to sustain the music. Stay still —— the music fades.

How to Play

🖐 Keep the Music Alive 🎵

The screen is divided into 4 sound zones. Move your hands into the zones to keep each one's life bar full. If you walk away, the music fades and the zone dies.

📍 Body Prompts 💃

A body part name will appear in a zone. Move that part into the zone before time runs out. Miss it and you lose a life. Complete it and you get a short breather before the next prompt.

✊ Snake 🐍

Make a fist to turn the snake right. Open your mouth to speed it up. Guide the snake to eat the fruit and gain an extra life.

❤️ Lives 💔

You start with 3 lives, up to a maximum of 5. Missing a body prompt costs 1 life. The game ends when all lives are gone and every zone goes silent.
